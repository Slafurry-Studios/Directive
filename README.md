## ABOUT
The Sector has been compromised — rogue hostiles are committing acts of terrorism against society, and you are the solution. As the pilot of the most advanced kinetic-redirection power suit, you're dropped into rooms full of enemies with one directive: clear them out, permanently. Unlike traditional bullet hells where projectiles vanish off-screen, every shot fired here stays in play, turning the battlefield itself into an ever-growing hazard you have to out-think as much as out-shoot.

## FEATURES
- **Kinetic compression** — projectiles shrink with each rebound, changing the threat they pose over time
- **Phase technology** — energy-based tech granting short windows of invulnerability
- **Persistent battlefield** — projectiles don't despawn off-screen; every shot fired is a lasting threat
- **Directive-based clearing** — rooms of enemies must be fully neutralized to progress
